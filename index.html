Certifications

Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP)

Gold standard for hands-on, practical penetration testing.

GIAC Reverse Engineering Malware (GREM)

Specialist certification that goes deep into the technical aspects of reverse engineering and malware analysis.

GIAC Certified Forensic Analyst (GCFA)

It is an advanced certification that validates a professional's ability to conduct in-depth incident investigations.

CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional)

Most globally recognized and respected certification for information security management.

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

Offered by the EC-Council, the CEH is a well-known certification that validates your knowledge of hacking tools and methodologies.
